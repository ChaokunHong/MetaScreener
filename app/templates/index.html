Performance & Accuracy

Based on validation studies with 4,230 articles from 18 institutions, MetaScreener demonstrates 95-97% sensitivity in identifying relevant studies. This means it rarely misses important papers. Specificity ranges from 85-92%, significantly reducing manual review workload.
MetaScreener enhances, not replaces, traditional methods. It accelerates the screening process while maintaining researcher oversight and decision-making authority. Final inclusion/exclusion decisions should always be validated by human experts.
MetaScreener provides detailed justifications for each decision, allowing you to review and override AI recommendations. The system is designed with high sensitivity to minimize false negatives (missing relevant papers), though this may increase false positives requiring manual review.
